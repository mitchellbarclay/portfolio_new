Confusion

Confusion is a short film that incorporates a mixture of stop-motion animation and live-action footage in order to create a surreal environment. The goal was to evoke the emotion of confusion in the viewer in a 30 second video.

Directed, written & animated by: Mitchell Barclay
Music by: Karunarathna Divulgane
Applications used: Adobe Premiere, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Photoshop


Android Tears

Android Tears is a music video that explores the theme of artificial intelligence and how that may change our perception of cosciousness and emotions as humans. The video follows the narrative of a robot that expresses feelings of lonliness, and uncertainty about the meaning of life.

Directed, written & animated by: Mitchell Barclay
Music & lyrics by: Mitchell Barclay
Applications used: Adobe Premiere, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Photoshop
