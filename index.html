MITCHELL BARCLAY

My name is Mitchell and I am a designer based in Wellington, New Zealand. I am currently studying media design at Victoria University of Wellington, and as of 2019 I am in my final year of study. My design skills cover a wide range of mediums. From video design (animation, editing, videography), graphic design, web design (coding HTML, CSS, Javascript), to 3D modelling. I have particular passion for animation and film.

I will try and let my work speak for itself so have a look around. If you are interested in contacting me or want to see my work on other platforms, the relevant links are below.
